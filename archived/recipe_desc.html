Types of Recipes

Breakfast

Traditional savory and sweet recipes that are great for a breakfast spread!

Examples include french toast, rice porridge, scrambles, and scones.

Desserts

Treat yourself with recipes that satisfy your sweet tooth!

Examples include muffins, cookies, and cakes.

Drinks

Enjoy a refreshing drink on a hot day or warm up with a hot drink on a cold day!

Examples include passionfruit green tea, berry smoothies, mocha coffee, and lemonade.

Meat & Tofu

Discover new styles, marinades, and dishes using meat and/or tofu!

Examples include chicken curry, pork rice, chipotle chicken, and mapo tofu.

Pasta & Noodles

Oodles and oodles of pasta and noodles!

Examples include black bean noodles, lasagna, and spicy noodle soup.

Seafood

Take a bite out of the ocean with some fresh seafood recipes!

Examples include sweet and sour shrimp, miso salmon, and poke bowls.

Snacks

Snacks that you can make ahead of time for a midday pick-me-up!

Examples include sweet potato fries, vegetable dip, granola bars.

Soups

Some delicious soup recipes that will warm your stomach and heart!

Examples include french onion soup, clam chowder, corn soup, and cauliflower soup.

Vegetables

Add some greens to your day with recipes that will make you want to eat your veggies!

Examples include salads, roasted brussel sprouts, and stir-fry napa cabbage.
