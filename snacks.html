Salsa

Serving Size: 2 cups


Ingredients:

  • 1 tomato
  • ¼ of a red onion
  • 1 bunch of cilantro (about ¾ cup)
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • ½ tsp of garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp of lime juice (about ½ a lime)

Step by Step:

  1. Dice up the tomato, red onion, and cilantro.
  2. Add in the salt, garlic powder, and lime juice.
  3. Cover and shake until throughly mixed.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before eating.
